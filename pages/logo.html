Housing Logos

Assignment

We were first told that we would be designing a logo for a housing buisness with an unknown name and began to research possibilities, then when we recieved the name we were to finish the logo.

Process

These logos took a fair amount of research and experimentation, leading to about five or so logos that were dropped from the project entirely. These three logos went through many versions until they reached their final result.

Result

The final result is a series of three potential, finalized logos for this housing buisness, ideal for the group to pick which one appeals to them the most.
