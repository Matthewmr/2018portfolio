LMC Poster

Assignment

The goal was to create an poster to encourage or intrest Los Medanos Collage students in the Art Department. Any medium could be protrayed for the department, leading to the decision of Digital Media being the centerpoint for the poster.

Process

The Project first began with several basic sketches. Once the main idea was sorted out, the concept was translated into Adobe illustrator and expanded upon. Several versions of the poster were created before the final product was ready.

Result

The result is a wire-frame / grid protruding from the poster, displaying a digital landscape guided by pencil which follows along a red carpet to a degree at the center of the grid.
