Zorn

Assignment

The Assignment was to recreate a photograph I had taken using only photoshop and varying thicknesses of line. The idea was to create a photo-realistic piece by utilizing different values and linework.

Process

The project took a while to complete due to the nature of shading exclusively with lines. There were no sketches needed as I first needed a proper picture then worked over it in photoshop in a form of tracing.

Result

The resulting image is quite nice to look at, as the composition and values really pop out the figures and make then appear to be more lifelike than a normal black and white drawing.
