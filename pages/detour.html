Another Detour

Assignment

The Goal was to create an intresting visual design/piece by using only black and white markers/ink.

Process

The assignment started out as a pencil on paper sketch as I mapped the various shapes and curves featured in the piece. Before marker was applied I selected an area where the color would invert and began using marker on the piece.

Result

The final result features many different bends and twists while also having solid and unique shapes to contrast with the overall flow of the piece. Another Detour was one of two 2D design pieces selected for the LMC 2017 spring art show.
